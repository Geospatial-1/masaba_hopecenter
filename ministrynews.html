Welcome to Masaba Hope Center Ministry

Sharing the love of Christ through service, compassion, and hope for every child and family.

Rev. John Mwita

Senior Pastor & Founder
Rev. John Mwita leads Masaba Hope Center Ministry with a vision to transform lives through faith, education, and compassion. His dedication to serving the vulnerable, especially children, has inspired many to join the mission of hope and restoration in Kuria and beyond.

"We believe every child deserves love, dignity, and a future. Thank you for being part of this journey of faith and service."
