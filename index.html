Our Mission

To provide hope, care, and opportunities for orphaned and vulnerable children in Kuria, Migori County, empowering them to lead successful lives and break the cycle of poverty.

Our Programs

We provide education, healthcare, and community support to change lives.

Message from Our Manager

Dear Friends and Supporters,

My name is Annastancia Mogesi Maroa, and I am the Manager of Masaba Hope Center, a dedicated children's home located in the heart of Kuria, Migori County. Our mission is to offer a safe haven for orphaned and vulnerable children, many of whom have been deeply affected by poverty, loss, and hardship.

We urgently need your help. Your donations will directly impact these children, providing them the chance to experience safety, nourishment, and the opportunity to build a better future.

Warm regards,
Annastancia Mogesi Maro
Manager, Masaba Hope Center
