Masaba Hope Center

Empowering Communities, Building Futures

Our Mission

To provide hope, care, and opportunities for orphaned and vulnerable children in Kuria, Migori County,Kenya empowering them to lead successful lives and break the cycle of poverty.

Our Programs

Providing education to underprivileged children in Kuria.

Operating in Kenya's Migori county,Kenya we focus on bridging the gap for orphans and vulnurable children in our midst access basic needs by partnering with
other partners and donors. There can nevery be a time like this when the need to help is needed the most. Welcome and feel free to partner with us in this journey

Offering the community relevant capacity building.

Our people benefit from the various programs that improve the quality of their lives. From rights against GBV to advocacy, we alleviate the voice of the voiceless
to enable our people live their lives with dignity.

Empowering women through skill development programs.

We help our members organise themselves in micro groups "Chamas" where we link them to financial providers so that they can access credit for their own development
we belive that a woman who is enabled can develop our society and change the community for good. We source financial aid so that the women and men in our group can access
financial literacy for their won improvement and development. Its through such activities that we can truely change our community
